Element X is an indie, roguelite, bullet hell game built in Unity3D and C# by a small group of colleagues from the University of Calgary. It has hundreds of upgrades to buff up your character to fight against enemies and bosses that grow infinitely as the player progresses through the game. Element X includes features such as a competitive scoreboard and epic sound effects and background music which makes gameplay more immersive.

After months of development, leading a team of beta testers for bug testing and balancing, collaborating with our composers [BackSamurai] and [GeoffLi] and our artist [KumoChaDesu], we were able to release our first game on STEAM which currently has hundreds of players competing for high scores.

A bomb defusing puzzle game built in C++ and Chai3D libraries for the Novint Falcon Haptic Device inspired by the VR game Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes by Steel Crate Games. Whether it's tactile feedback from buttons or the texture of wood of a table, the Novint Falcon simulates force feedback to allow the user to feel his or her objects in 3D space. Our bomb has some small puzzles on it such as the Simon Says Button Sequence and Braille codes to give the users clues for defusing the bomb before time runs out!

This project implements various forms of non-photorealistic rendering methods. We created several different shaders in the graphics pipeline to apply methods such as cartoon shading, hatch and stipple sketching to render our 3D scenes in real time.

Built in C++ and OpenGL Libraries, the 3D Character Creator applies Object Oriented Programming concepts to implement half-edge data structures and subdivision methods that lets the user easily morph a mesh to his or her desires. For example, If your animated character recognizable facial features such as wrinkles, high cheekbones or an abnormally big nose, users can simply select the regions on the mesh and push and pull them out. The user can subdivide the region for a higher resolution and a smoother look on the mesh. Lastly, the user is also able to import and export meshes as .obj files for use in other programs such as Blender or Maya3D.
